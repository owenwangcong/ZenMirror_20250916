Revolutionary Radar Technology

Millimeter wave radar achieves clinical-grade physiological monitoring without any physical contact, creating an entirely new paradigm for meditation technology.

Five Precise Data Parameters

ZenMirror's millimeter wave radar captures five distinct physiological data streams simultaneously, providing unprecedented insight into your meditation state.

Breathing Phase (呼吸相位) - Radians

Continuous phase measurement of chest wall displacement during breathing cycles. Each breath creates a sinusoidal wave pattern tracked in real-time.

Example: 1.57 radians = peak inhalation

Heartbeat Phase (心跳相位) - Radians

High-frequency phase measurements from cardiac mechanical activity, capturing micro-movements from heart contractions through chest wall.

Example: 0.1-0.3 radian changes per heartbeat

Body Distance - Centimeters

Distance measurement tracking body movements and postural changes during meditation sessions for comprehensive analysis.

Example: 80cm baseline ±2cm movement range

HRV Analysis (RMSSD & SDNN)

RMSSD: Parasympathetic activity indicator
SDNN: Overall autonomic balance measure

RMSSD >50ms = excellent relaxation

Respiratory Sinus Arrhythmia (RSA)

Heart rate acceleration/deceleration with breathing patterns. RSA amplitude indicates meditation depth and emotional regulation.

RSA 15+ BPM = deep contemplative state
