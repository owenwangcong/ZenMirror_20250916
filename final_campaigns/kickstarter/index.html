Understanding Your Data

HRV (Heart Rate Variability)

Measures the variation in time between heartbeats, indicating nervous system flexibility and resilience.

RSA (Respiratory Sinus Arrhythmia)

Shows how heart rate naturally speeds up during inhalation and slows during exhalation.

Breath Coherence

Tracks the harmony between breathing rhythm and heart rate patterns during meditation.

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is contactless monitoring?

Our millimeter wave radar technology achieves clinical-grade accuracy for heart rate and breathing patterns, with precision comparable to direct contact sensors.

Is the radar technology safe?

Absolutely. The power levels used are extremely low – thousands of times lower than your smartphone. It's completely safe for continuous use.

Can it work through clothing?

Yes! ZenMirror works perfectly through normal clothing and even light blankets, making it ideal for comfortable meditation practice.

What's the range and positioning requirements?

ZenMirror works optimally at 0.5-2 meters distance and can accommodate various meditation postures and positions.

Do I need internet connectivity?

Basic functionality works offline. Internet connection enables mentor updates, progress sync, and community features.

How do mentor personalities adapt to individuals?

Each mentor uses machine learning to understand your response patterns and adjusts guidance timing, tone, and content for maximum resonance.
