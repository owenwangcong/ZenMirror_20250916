How Contactless Sensing Works

ZenMirror uses sophisticated millimeter wave radar technology that detects micro-movements of your chest wall during breathing and heartbeat cycles. This enables precise monitoring of vital signs without any physical contact.

Frequency Range: 60-64 GHz millimeter wave
Detection Range: 0.5 - 2.5 meters optimal
Accuracy: Clinical-grade precision (¬±2 BPM heart rate)
Safety: 1000x lower power than smartphone

Advanced Physiological Analysis

Heart Rate Monitoring

Detects cardiac mechanical activity through chest wall micro-movements. Provides continuous, beat-to-beat heart rate measurement with clinical accuracy.

Real-time heart rate tracking

Respiratory Analysis

Monitors breathing frequency and depth through ribcage expansion patterns. Tracks breathing phase and coherence for optimal meditation timing.

Breathing pattern analysis

Heart Rate Variability (HRV)

Calculates RMSSD and SDNN metrics to assess autonomic nervous system balance and stress resilience. Key indicator of meditation quality.

HRV trend analysis

Respiratory Sinus Arrhythmia (RSA)

Measures heart rate-breathing synchronization. High RSA indicates deep relaxation and optimal meditation states.

RSA coherence measurement

Body Movement Detection

Tracks stillness and micro-movements during meditation. Helps assess physical comfort and meditation posture quality.

Stillness quality tracking

AI-Powered Integration

Machine learning algorithms combine all metrics to generate meditation quality scores and personalized feedback recommendations.

Privacy & Security by Design

Non-Imaging Technology

Millimeter wave radar detects only movement, not images. Completely preserves visual privacy while providing precise physiological data.

Local Data Processing

All sensitive physiological data is processed locally on your device. Only anonymized insights are optionally shared for improvement.

End-to-End Encryption

All data transmission uses military-grade encryption. Your meditation data remains completely private and secure.

Offline Operation

Core functionality works completely offline. Internet connection only needed for updates and optional cloud sync.
